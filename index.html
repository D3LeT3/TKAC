Welcome to The Kind Animal Collective

I'm Neha—founder and force-free dog trainer based in Sydney. Meet Roggie too—we believe training should feel good for dogs and humans.

I help busy individuals and families raise calm, well-mannered dogs without the overwhelm—whether you're starting with a new puppy or working through early behaviour challenges.

With a background in teaching and behaviour-focused training, I break everything into simple, practical steps that work in real homes and real neighbourhoods.

Everything I do is rooted in kindness.

FAQ

How do I contact you?

Send a message using the form below. You'll usually hear back within 1–2 days by email to schedule your free 30-minute online consult (Zoom, or Teams, FaceTime, or WhatsApp video if you prefer).

What happens in the free consult?

It's a relaxed chat to meet each other, learn about your dog, and explore what support you need. I'll explain how I work—no pressure. This consult is not a full assessment; if we decide to work together, I'll visit in person, observe your dog, and build a tailored training plan.

Do you offer tailored packages?

Yes. I'm happy to discuss packages shaped around your goals and training plan.

Do you provide pet sitting?

Yes—day care and pet hosting at my home for small–medium dogs, plus house visits for dogs and cats. See the Madpaws listing for details and pricing.
